# Player Presentation Sprite Prompts

## victory_pose

Use `assets/characters/player/presentation/reference/player_existing_reference_board.png` as the strict identity and style reference for this exact playable character.

Create a 6-frame sprite sheet, exactly 3 columns x 2 rows, side-view right-facing, equal-size cells.

The brown tabby cat warrior performs a cooler boss-defeat stage-clear victory pose in the spirit of a 90s arcade fighting game: low ready stance, staff flourish upward, confident step-in with the purple staff tip catching light, cloak snap / hero turn, then a final staff-across-shoulder pose. The last two frames should be nearly still and usable as a hold pose.

Keep the same player identity as the existing KittyBrawl hero: short stout compact brown tabby cat proportions, round face, yellow eyes, white muzzle and paws, pink inner ears, dark brown/black ragged cloak, leather wraps, small brass/gold ornaments, rope belt, striped tail thickness, determined expression, wooden staff with purple magical tip.

Style: 90s arcade pixel art, side-view action platformer sprite, crisp hard pixel edges, high-detail hand-authored pixel clusters, dark fantasy palette, readable at gameplay scale.

Technical constraints: solid #FF00FF chroma-key background, no shadows, no gradients, no floor plane, no text, no UI, no extra characters, no modern vector style, no 3D render. Keep body scale consistent with player combat/super sheets. Keep feet on one stable ground line except minor cloak motion. Nothing may touch or cross cell edges. Avoid purple cloak, tall/slim body drift, redesigned face, redesigned outfit, or new weapon design.
