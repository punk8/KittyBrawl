# Player Cat-Form Sprite Prompts

These prompts define the temporary cat-form animations used by the rat boss special attacks.

## Shared Style Lock

Use the existing player cat warrior as the identity reference, but transform the character into a natural four-legged cat form. Keep the same tabby fur pattern, golden eyes, white muzzle/chest/paws, and slightly stern expression. Preserve the project's 1990s arcade pixel-art look: hand-painted dithering, crisp black pixel outlines, warm brown shadows, amber highlights, and transparent-friendly solid magenta background.

The cat should read as the same hero after being hypnotized, not a new character. Do not include the staff, cloak, armor, UI, scenery, shadows, or background props unless the individual action asks for a small bait prop.

## Action Set

### cat_run

Six-frame side-view run cycle of the player transformed into a four-legged tabby cat, moving eagerly forward under hypnosis. Body low and stretched, tail balancing, paws cycling clearly.

Suggested output: `3 x 2` sheet, `128 x 128` frame cells, 6 frames.

### cat_eat

Four-frame loop of the cat stopping and eating cat food on the ground, calm and obedient. Head dips down, small chew motion, tail relaxed.

Suggested output: `2 x 2` sheet, `128 x 128` frame cells, 4 frames.

### cat_pounce

Six-frame pounce sequence toward a paper ball: crouch, leap, paws extended, landing posture. Keep the pose readable at small canvas scale.

Suggested output: `3 x 2` sheet, `128 x 128` frame cells, 6 frames.

### cat_stunned

Four-frame dazed/stunned loop after the boss trick succeeds. Cat low to the ground, soft dizzy posture, brief loss of control.

Suggested output: `2 x 2` sheet, `128 x 128` frame cells, 4 frames.

### cat_knockback

Six-frame knockback sequence after being kicked: impact recoil, airborne backward motion, landing tumble. No gore.

Suggested output: `3 x 2` sheet, `128 x 128` frame cells, 6 frames.

### cat_recover

Four-frame recovery sequence from submissive cat form back toward player control: shake head, brace paws, regain alert expression.

Suggested output: `2 x 2` sheet, `128 x 128` frame cells, 4 frames.
