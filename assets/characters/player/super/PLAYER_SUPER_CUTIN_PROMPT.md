# Player Super Cut-In Prompt

`assets/characters/player/super/player_super_cutin.png`

## Prompt

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: 2D fighting-game super move character cut-in banner for a browser canvas game
Primary request: Create a dramatic horizontal super move cut-in image of the Kitty Brawl hero cat warrior.

Subject:
The same hero cat from the existing game sprites: stout brown tabby cat warrior, golden eyes, white muzzle and chest, small rounded ears, stern focused expression, dark ragged cloak, layered dark leather/cloth armor, bandaged forearms, wooden staff with purple crystal head. Preserve the character identity, palette, costume, and weapon details from the current player sprite sheets.

Composition/framing:
Wide cinematic horizontal banner, approximately 16:5 aspect ratio. The cat hero appears as a large face and upper-body close-up occupying the left 60% of the frame, angled slightly toward the right, eyes looking fiercely to the right. Include the staff diagonally across the lower foreground with the purple crystal visible. Leave the right 35-40% as dark energetic negative space so the game can slide the cut-in across the screen. Do not show the full body.

Style/medium:
High-quality 16-bit SNES / Sega Genesis era pixel art, dark fantasy action game style, crisp pixel clusters, strong dark outline, hand-painted pixel shading, no smooth modern vector look, no anime cel shading, no photorealism.

Scene/backdrop:
Dark purple and black magical burst background, speed lines, crescent slash energy, small gold sparks, subtle crystal glow. This is a complete rectangular cut-in banner with its own background, not a transparent cutout.

Lighting/mood:
Intense battle-ready mood. Purple rim light from the crystal staff, warm gold highlights in the eyes, high contrast, dramatic super move impact.

Color palette:
Dark browns, charcoal cloak, warm tan muzzle and paws, gold eyes, muted brass ornaments, saturated purple crystal energy, black shadows. Keep colors compatible with the existing Kitty Brawl assets.

Constraints:
No text, no logo, no watermark, no UI, no numbers, no speech bubble. No extra characters, no rats, no enemies. Do not make the cat cute or round; keep it stern, heavy, battle-ready, and consistent with the sprite identity. Avoid cropped-off eyes. Avoid blurry painterly rendering. Avoid 3D rendering. Avoid plain flat background.
```

## QC Checklist

- The hero reads as the same cat warrior as the existing player sprite.
- The image is a wide rectangular cut-in banner, not a full-body pose.
- Face and upper torso are large enough to read quickly during a 0.6-0.8 second super activation.
- Right side has enough energetic negative space for slide-in motion.
- No text, logo, watermark, UI, extra characters, or enemies.
- Pixel-art style matches the current 16-bit dark fantasy game assets.
