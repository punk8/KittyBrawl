# Prototype Asset Requirements

This file tracks the minimum assets needed for the first playable Kitty Brawl prototype:
one screen, one controllable cat warrior, movement, jumping, normal attack, super move,
one or two enemies, enemy hit reactions, and enemy death.

## Player Required

- `characters/player/movement/idle/sheet-transparent.png`
- `characters/player/movement/run/sheet-transparent.png`
- `characters/player/movement/jump_fall/sheet-transparent.png`
- `characters/player/combat/attack_01/sheet-transparent.png`
- `characters/player/combat/hurt/sheet-transparent.png`
- `characters/player/super/super_activation/sheet-transparent.png`
- `characters/player/super/super_attack/sheet-transparent.png`

## Player Nice To Have

- `characters/player/combat/death/sheet-transparent.png`

## Effects Required

- `effects/player/slash_fx/sheet-transparent.png`
- `effects/player/hit_spark/sheet-transparent.png`
- `effects/player/super_impact_fx/sheet-transparent.png`
- `effects/player/super_background_plate/`

## Enemy Required

- One small enemy idle sheet.
- One small enemy walk or patrol sheet.
- One small enemy hit sheet.
- One small enemy death sheet.

## Level Required

- One static or parallax background.
- One small floor/platform tileset.
- One simple level layout or collision map.

## UI Required

- Player health bar.
- Super meter.

## Data Required

- Player animation config.
- Player hitbox and hurtbox config.
- Enemy animation config.
- Enemy hitbox and hurtbox config.
- One-screen level config.

## Audio Required Later

- Jump.
- Land.
- Normal attack.
- Hit.
- Enemy death.
- Super activation.
- Super impact.
